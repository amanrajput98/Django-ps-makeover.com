ABOUT Me

I'm Priyanka Singh, Freelance Professional Makeup Artist, located in Canton, Michigan.

Hello! Thank you for stopping by! My craving and talent for the art and craft of makeup is the quintessential goal for continuing the profession as a career. I am looking ahead to creating some magic with you!
